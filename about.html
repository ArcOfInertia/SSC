About STI College Lucena Student Council

Empowering students, fostering leadership, and building a vibrant campus community.

Our Mission

The STI College Lucena Student Council is dedicated to representing the voice of every student, promoting academic excellence, and fostering a positive campus environment that encourages leadership, innovation, and community engagement.

Our Vision

To be the leading student organization that empowers STI College Lucena students to become responsible leaders, active citizens, and successful professionals who contribute positively to society.

Our Values

  • Leadership: Developing and nurturing student leaders
  • Excellence: Promoting academic and personal excellence
  • Integrity: Maintaining honesty and ethical conduct
  • Service: Serving the student body and community
  • Innovation: Embracing new ideas and approaches
